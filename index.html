About Lynn

Rooted in the
Red River Valley

Lynn Pynn is a licensed Realtor® with Crary Real Estate in Grand Forks, North Dakota, serving buyers and sellers across both North Dakota and Minnesota. With a bachelor's degree in business administration and nearly two decades of experience as a paralegal, Lynn brings a unique level of professionalism, contract knowledge, and attention to detail that sets her apart.

Her approach is simple: clients come first, every time. Lynn takes the time to understand what matters most and works tirelessly to make sure every client feels informed and confident from start to finish — whether it's their first transaction or their tenth.

From residential and first-time homebuyers to land, acreage, and commercial properties, Lynn works across all property types with the same dedication to her clients' best interests.
