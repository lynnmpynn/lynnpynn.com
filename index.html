About Lynn

Rooted in the
Red River Valley

Lynn Pynn is a licensed real estate agent with Crary Real Estate in Grand Forks, North Dakota, serving buyers and sellers across both North Dakota and Minnesota. A longtime resident of the greater Grand Forks area, Lynn brings a genuine understanding of the local market — and a deep commitment to making sure every client feels informed, heard, and confident at every step.

Her approach is straightforward: real estate is one of the biggest decisions people make, and they deserve an agent who treats it that way. Lynn takes the time to understand what matters most to her clients — their needs, their concerns, their timeline — and works tirelessly on their behalf, whether it's their first transaction or their tenth.

From residential and first-time homebuyers to land, acreage, and commercial properties, Lynn works across all property types with the same attention to detail and dedication to her clients' best interests.
